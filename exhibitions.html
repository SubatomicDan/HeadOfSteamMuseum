Track Across Time

October 16th, 2013

Arising from an exhibition originally commissioned by the NRM 'Locomotion' three local artists under the banner of 'Cluster' present a unique exhibition of contemporary art within Head of Steam - Darlington Railway Museum, inspired by the area's railway heritage. Cluster members Carolyn Corfield, Vera Hynes and Graeme Rowatt have embraced the concept of designing site specific exhibitions to challenge their individual skills, their aesthetic interaction with each other and in this case to acknowledge events which impacted to such extent on the local community. Displayed amongst the permanent exhibits within North Road Museum their original pieces aim to enhance the visitor experience by delivering an evocative journey through the artist's imagination. Women and the Great War

March 16th, 2014

We are happy to announce that The History Wardrobe will be visiting the museum on Sunday 16th March 2014 to perform their newest performance 'Women and the Great War'. World War One had a catastrophic impact on the lives of millions, both on the battlefield and on the Home Front too. It was also a time of tremendous social upheaval, bringing new opportunities and freedoms to many women, all of which had a dramatic impact on fashions. Tickets include entry to the museum and a complimentary tea/coffee
