Here's what events are currently taking place at the museum

Santa at the Station

December 8th, 2013

Meet Santa in our magical grotto and receive a Christmas present, entertainment includes mini train ride, arts and crafts. Entrance fee applies and booking is essential, contact museum.

Christmas Gathering

December 5th, 2013

Taking place at 1:45pm. The Friends and Museum Staff Christmas Gathering. Friends of the museum are invited to the museum for a buffet lunch, quiz and a glass of mulled wine as the museum thanks them for another year of assistance. Contact the museum if you would like to become a member.

Afternoon Lectures

November 7th, 2013

"The Origins of the North Eastern Railway" - a lecture by Charles McNab. Organised by the Friends of the Museum. Free to members of the Friends, non members welcome (please telephone the museum for membership or price details).

Scooby Doo and Little Boo!

October 27th, 2013

Come along to our specially decorated museum and meet the canine crime-solving detective! Normal museum entrance fee applies.
